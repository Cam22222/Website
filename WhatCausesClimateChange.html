Cause

The causes of climate change are very serious. They have many bad effects on the world that should not be ingorned. Some of them are higher temperatures, more severe storms, not enough food, and loss of species. People do things everyday to make climate change worse, just turning on your light or taking a family flight for a fun vacation or even just using your gas car to get around. All of these things go to making climate change worse.

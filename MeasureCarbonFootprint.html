Ways to Reduce Carbon Footprint

  • Don't use plastic water bottles
  • Instead of using a car to travel consider using bikes or walking
  • Turn off/Unplug devices or lights
  • Choose organic food or food with little waste
  • Turn off AC or Heat in your home when your not using it
